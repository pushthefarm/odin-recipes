Seared Ahi Tuna

Description

Let's get one thing straight, this ain't canned tuna! You have to buy this at your grocery store, along with a few other ingredients. It's simple to make, and your friends will think you're a badass cook, and they'll make you cook at every gathering. And that's okay, granted that they do the dishes!

Ingredients

    T
  • Two 5 oz Ahi Tuna Steaks
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns

Directions

    Pat the tuna steaks dry and season both sides with salt and cayenne pepper.
  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add olive oil & peppercorns. Cook until the peppercorns soften & pop, which is around 5 minutes.
  3. Gently place seasoned tuna in the skillet and cook until you're satisfied with it's doneness. This is all on personal preference, but the more medium-rare the steak, the better!
  4. Slice the tuna however many pieces you like, but I'm not going to share!
